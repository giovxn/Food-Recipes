Ribeye Steak Recipe

Ribeye Steak

Description

The ribeye steak requires a well cut piece of cow meat that is cut near the cow's beef, this can be purchased at your local grocery store or at a bucther shop. Seasoning such as salt, butter and thyme helps boost the flavor of the meat to give it a rich taste!

Side dishes such as mashed potato, rice, fries, corn, etc are an option for a more enjoyable experience for you! These recipes can be access throughhere.

Ingredients

  • 1 Ribeye Steak
  • 3-4 tsp Salt
  • Large cube of Butter
  • Thyme or Rosemary
  • 2 or 3 cloves of Garlic

Steps

  1. We take our Ribeye Steak (defrosted) and season it with our salt. Make sure to apply a generous (3-4 tsp) even coating of seasoning.
  2. Take a cast-iron pan and add a large healthy cube of butter, our cloves of garlic and our Thyme or Rosemary
  3. Fire up our stove by applying high to medium heat, let the butter melt for about 3-4 minutes
  4. Then we take our Ribeye Steak and gently place it on the cast-iron pan from top to bottom (dont be afraid of the sizzling sound!)
  5. Leave the steak there for 5-7 minutes before flipping it over
  6. Once flipped, we angle our skillet towards ourself on an approximate 30-degree angle, keep the steak at the top of the skillet (make sure not to spill any butter!)
  7. Take a spoon and scoop and pour the butter at the bottom of the pan to the steak, this method is called basting, depending on how we want our steak done we baste the steak til our steak reaches 125 degrees of internal temperature
  8. Once temperature is up to your standards, take it out of the pan and serve on a plate!
